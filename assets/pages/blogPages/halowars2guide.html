Halo Wars 2 Guide

I recommend you check out this link! If you're feeling lazy, here's a quick description:

Halo Wars 2 is the second installation of the Xbox RTS (Real time strategy) Halo Wars. Players can build up an army to capture objectives or destroy the enemy base in this RTS.
Resources:
All leader abilities

Strategy 1: Anders Sentinel Sieging (anti-infantry start)

Deathmatch 2v2 / 3v3

Starting Base Build Order:

Supply Pad (upgrade this ASAP) -> Generator (upgrade this ASAP) -> Supply Pad -> Barracks -> Generator -> Upgrade Base -> Garage -> Garage

Leader Ability Upgrade Order

R&D 1 -> Protector Sentinel -> Ark Defense 1 -> Sentinel Beacon -> Sentinel Synergy -> Retriever Sentinel -> (Rest is up to you. Most important part here is getting the Protector Sentinel and Sentinel Beacon)

Action Order
  1. Queue up your supply pad, generator, and second supply pad.
  2. Send your two starting marines out to the middle of the map to "steal" resources away from the opposite team. The success of this will depend on the experience of the opposite team, of course.
  3. While your marines are out and about, upgrade your first supply pad as soon as it's up, and upgrade your first generator whenever you can.
  4. Queue up a jackrabbit for resources, a barracks for the rush, and another generator for the 5th base slot.
  5. Continue gathering resources and start queueing up snipers and flamethrowers.
  6. Around the 5:00 minute mark is when you should be meeting up with the rest of your team to attack one of the bases. Take a second base with additional supply generators and an armory before sending all your units off.
  7. While attacking, continuously spit out snipers/flamethrowers/sentinels (You should have the Protector Sentinel leader power at this point).
  8. At this point, one of two things will happen.
    1. You will wipe out all of their bases and won the game at around the 10 minute mark.
    2. They will have countered your rush, and will be forced to move into the mid/late game.
  9. In the case of 8B, start using you garages to build Kodiaks and continue building Sentinels.
  10. Set up the Kodiaks near choke points where you think the enemy team will go through.
  11. Optional: Depending on the game, you may also need to build wolverines.
  12. If you're able to get an offensive set up, i.e. set up Kodiaks within firing range of the enemy base, do so and make sure you have the Sentinel Beacon leader power, having this power is critical to this strategy.
  13. Use your sentinels to give your Kodiaks vision of some target you are trying to hit, and let the Sentinel Beacon + Kodiak Sieging do its magic.
  14. Adjust the amount of Kodiaks/Sentinel/Wolverines as you see fit.
  15. Win!
